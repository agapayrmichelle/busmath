Understanding Salary, Wages, Commission

Salary: Fixed amount paid regularly (monthly/yearly). Example: ‚Ç±20,000/month.

Wages: Paid based on hours or days worked. Formula: Wages = Hourly Rate √ó Hours Worked or Daily Wage √ó Days Worked

Commission: Payment based on sales. Formula: Commission = Sales √ó Commission Rate

Tips: Always identify if pay is fixed (salary), time-based (wages), or performance-based (commission).
